Visa

Check if You Need a Visa

If you plan to attend PyConFR 2026 in Biarritz, please check if you need a visa to enter France and apply as early as possible.

France is part of the Schengen area. If you already have a valid Schengen visa, you do not need to apply for an additional one. If in doubt, please contact your consulate or embassy.

You can also visit the France Visas website to check if you need a visa.

Invitation Letter

If you need a visa, we can provide an invitation letter for your application.

Invitation letter requests should be sent via email to secretaire@afpy.org. Please include the following information in your request:

  • Title (Mr./Ms.),
  • Full name,
  • Date of birth,
  • Nationality,
  • Passport number,
  • Title of your presentation at the event (if applicable),
  • Amount of the grant provided by AFPy (if applicable),
  • Name of the sponsoring company you represent (if applicable).

Getting to the Campus

By Public Transport

The school is located on Izarbel technopole which is served by bus (Txik Txak) line 8.

By Train

The closest train station is Biarritz one, linked to Bordeaux TGV (high-speed train) station and then to the main French lines.

From Biarritz train station, you can take bus line 8 to Izarbel stop to get to the school in about 20 minutes.

By Coach

Biarritz is directly reachable from dozens of towns in France and Europe by FlixBus Coach.

By Car

Izarbel technopole is close to A63 highway, next to exit nulmber 4.

You're coming by car and you have room? Consider carsharing :).

By Plane

From Biarritz airport, you can take bus line 8 to Izarbel stop to get to the school in about 15 minutes.
